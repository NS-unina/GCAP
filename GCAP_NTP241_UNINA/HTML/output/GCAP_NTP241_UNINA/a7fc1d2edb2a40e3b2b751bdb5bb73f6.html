Control Action

Violated Constraints

  • (SC-01) The drone shall initiate controlled emergency procedures upon detection of flight instability.
  • (SC-04) The autopilot shall prevent climb commands exceeding the altitude ceiling.
  • (SC-02) The autopilot shall enforce geofencing limits based on predefined no-fly zones.

Countermeasures

  • The Radio Control shall ensure that control commands are within valid operational limits before transmission.
  • The Radio Control unit shall verify signal integrity before transmitting commands to the Autopilot.

Hazards

  • (H-01) Uncontrolled Emergency Landing
  • (H-02) Unintentional entry into unauthorized areas
  • (H-04) Violation of the operational altitude ceiling

Unsafe Control Actions

  • (UCA-02) The RC transmits corrupted or inaccurate commands due to signal degradation or interface error.
