  • (H-01) Uncontrolled Emergency Landing

  • (SC-01) The drone shall initiate controlled emergency procedures upon detection of flight instability.
  • (SC-02) The autopilot shall enforce geofencing limits based on predefined no-fly zones.
  • (SC-03) The drone shall switch to inertial navigation and trigger RTH (Return-To-Home) upon GPS signal loss.
  • (SC-04) The autopilot shall prevent climb commands exceeding the altitude ceiling.
  • (SC-05) Navigation calculations shall be validated against sensor fusion confidence thresholds.
  • (SC-06) The drone shall initiate auto-hover or RTH when radio control is lost.
  • (SC-07) Critical flight decisions shall not be executed without validated telemetry input.
  • (SC-08) Information among drone components shall happen across secure channels
